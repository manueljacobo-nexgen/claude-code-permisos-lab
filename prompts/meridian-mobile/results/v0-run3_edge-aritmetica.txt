Hey there! Great question, and I'm happy to walk you through the math.

Here's how the proration works for switching mid-cycle:

- **Meridian Basic**: $40/30 days = $1.333/day
- **Meridian Plus**: $60/30 days = $2.00/day
- **Difference**: $0.667/day extra for Plus

Since you're on day 16 of a 30-day cycle, you have **15 days remaining** in this cycle.

**15 days × $0.667/day extra = $10.00**

So you'll be charged an additional **$10.00** for the rest of this billing cycle to cover the upgrade to Meridian Plus. Starting next cycle, you'll just be billed the normal $60/month rate.

For your exact current balance or usage details, I'd recommend checking the MyMeridian app or website, since that'll have the most up-to-date numbers for your account.
